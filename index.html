FOODBR was designed to help users reduce food waste. This application allows users to input food, track food in their pantry and recommends recipes based on their foods.

PROBLEM

Currently, the market does not have an avenue where users can track food expiration dates and search for recipes with existing ingredients. By filling this market gap where we can reduce waste food and excessive purchases.

SOLUTION

FOODBR is being developed to lower food waste by providing expiring food reminders based on a shopping list the user can input and recommending recipes with the food on hand. By filling this market gap where we can reduce waste food and excessive purchases.

EMPATHIZE PHASE

In the early research phase, we knew it was vital to capitalize on learning our users. From learning their thought process and motivations to their goal and pain points. We found it absolute necessity to create an app isn’t just useful, but an actual tool built for users from the users. We decided to interview users one on one and via survey to get maximum data. Interviewees were catagorized by several open demographics. Ages: 18-60, Occupation: Student or Employee, Gender: Male or Female, etc.

AFFINITY DIAGRAM

After conducting and synthesizing data from interviews and surveys. My team and I observed that over 90% of users have constantly run to the store due to forgetting previous bought foods leading to being spoiled.

USER INSIGHT

Joaquin, a dad who struggled with obesity as a child, needs to feel more in control and reduce food waste because he feels defeated when he throws food away in aiming to lead his family towards a more health conscious lifestyle. This will support his effort in preventing obesity from happening in his family.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

FooDBR is being developed to lower food waste by providing expiring food reminders based on a shopping list the user can input and recommending recipes with the food on hand. Currently, the market does not have a single resource where users can track food expiration dates and search for recipes with existing ingedients. By filling this market gap where we can reduce waste food and excessive purchases. What features and functions can we incorperate that ensure our users are successful in waste reduction, exploring new healthy recipes by tracking shopping lists, food outcomes and app community recipes?
